Series 2021 6 Episodes Superhero

“The Falcon and the Winter Soldier” follows Sam Wilson struggling to take up the mantle of Captain America. Along for the ride is Bucky, who is going through his own issues from his past life as the brainwashed assassin known as the Winter Soldier.
Together, the two battle a radicalized group of super soldiers known as the Flag Smashers, the new-faux Captain America John Walker and Zemo, the methodical villain who tore the Avengers apart in “Captain America: Civil War.” Sharon Carter returns as the former S.H.I.E.L.D. and CIA agent hiding in exile as the mysterious Power Broker.
